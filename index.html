A brief history

On raspberry Pi, technology and coding

Raspberry Pi is a mini-computer that is so small, it could fit into your pocket . It is a low-cost device that you can be hooked up to a TV or a computer monitor. It can be used by people of all different ages to explore computing, design different projects and to code. You can also use it with Scratch and Python.  Almost anything that your normal computer can do, the Raspberry Pi can do. It is a series of small single size boards. The Raspberry Pi also uses a regular keyboard and mouse.

  • The Raspberry Pi was invented in May 2009.
  • Founders: Eben Upton, Alan Mycroft, David Braben, Pete Lomas, Jack Lang, Rob Mullins.
  • Raspberry Pi has 135 members.
  • The board of trustees for Raspberry Pi was assembled in 2008.
  • The Raspberry Pi logo was invented in October 2011, Raspberry Pi held an open competition for the public to design the logo for them. They made a short list of six of the best design's and the judging took serval day's. The designer of the Raspberry Pi logo is called Paul Beech.
