So how can tech help with mental health?

The last few years we have seen a good changes in mental health. People are now talking about it more and asking for help. Although this is good, the numbers of those in need of mental health treatment is still very high.

Emotion artificial intelligence (emotion AI) is a new and positive technology that offers another option for supporting people with resources and monitoring their well-being. This technology can analyze things such as voices, writing, and facial expressions of people and react to this -- just like a human would. This is extremely useful to healthcare professionals, and is getting better and improving all the time.

Emotion AI can free up doctors to work more with their patients by analyzing patient records and generating reports, handling administrative tasks, and even assisting with diagnosis. Emotion AI can also help patients create awareness of their emotional states and regulate their emotions better under stressful conditions.​

Emotion AI for Chatbots Delivering Personalized Therapy

Chatting with a computer may not be the first thing that pops into your mind for therapy, but for many patients, interacting with a chatbot emotion AI can really help. There are several companies developing avatars and assistive robots to provide therapeutic options for patients.​

Emotion AI technology can do a number of things when it comes to providing treatment for mental illnesses. It may not seem that helpful chatting to a robot but for many people it has been proven to be found extremely helpful.​

Most important, emotion AI technology automates talk therapy and accessibility, enabling broader access to mental health treatments, which is one of the most expensive and time-intensive forms of treatment.​

AI chatbots for personalized therapy

It may not seem like it, but for many patients, interacting with a chatbot powered by emotion AI can have huge benefits. There are several companies developing avatars and assistive robots to provide therapeutic options for patients now.
It has been shown that many people interact with avatars the way they would with other humans. Some patients feel more comfortable sharing their difficulties with a machine rather than another person. Some patients have a difficult time feeling comfortable in a therapy room, an emotion AI-powered chatbot can make people feel more comfortable.  The chatbot creates an emotionally safe place for sharing information.
